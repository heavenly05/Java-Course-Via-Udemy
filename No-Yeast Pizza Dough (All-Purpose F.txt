No-Yeast Pizza Dough (All-Purpose Flour):
If you don’t have bread flour, you can use all-purpose flour for this recipe.
Ingredients:
1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
½ teaspoon salt
½ cup fat-free milk
1 table spoon garlic powder
2 tablespoons olive oil
Instructions:
Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
Stir in milk and olive oil until a soft dough forms.
Knead briefly, then shape into a ball.
Let it sit for 10 minutes

roll it out and make it into a beveled circle

on the pizza crust butter the edges with melted butter mixed with minced garlic or whateverkind of garlic seasonign you have on hand and sprinkle specs of cheese, some garlic powder, 

sprinkle herbs in the center before adding condiments

Simple No-Cook Tomato Sauce:

Ingredients:

High-quality canned crushed tomatoes
Olive oil
2 cloves of garlic (grated) //  garlic powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon of garlic powder
Instructions:
Open the can of crushed tomatoes.
Add a few tablespoons of olive oil, grated garlic, and salt.
Stir to combine.
Use it right away or let it sit in the fridge for a few hours to mellow the garlic flavor.
This sauce pairs wonderfully with pizza3.
